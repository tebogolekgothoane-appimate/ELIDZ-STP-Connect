## 1. THE CORTEX HUB

### Description
A technology incubator and accelerator aimed at helping young entrepreneurs build products, teams, and scalable businesses. Located in East London, South Africa, dedicated to supporting startups that develop socially impactful solutions.

### Current Opportunities

#### Programs:
1. **Pre-Incubation Program**
   - Equips early-stage SMMEs with foundational skills
   - Prepares startups for Incubation phase
   - Provides technical and business knowledge
   - Application: https://forms.gle/nq2umWk67fL9i1SV9

2. **Incubation Program** (12 months)
   - Launchpad for aspiring tech entrepreneurs
   - Business planning and product development
   - Hands-on guidance and access to state-of-the-art facilities
   - Transforms innovative ideas into viable business models
   - Application: https://forms.gle/nq2umWk67fL9i1SV9

3. **Acceleration Program** (12 months)
   - Intensive program for startups ready to scale
   - Weekly sprint objectives
   - Mentorship from industry experts
   - Direct customer engagement
   - Focus on rapid growth, investor attraction, and market expansion
   - Application: https://forms.gle/nq2umWk67fL9i1SV9

### Products & Capabilities

#### Specialization Labs:
1. **Optic Fibre Lab**
   - Foundational and advanced fiber optics training
   - Certifications: CFOT, FOS/D, CPCT
   - Skills: Design and manage fiber optic networks

2. **Bare Metal as a Service Lab**
   - Data center automation
   - Managing physical servers in dedicated environments
   - Non-virtualized resources
   - Practical experience with server cabinets

3. **Automotive Ethernet Lab**
   - Specialized training in automotive networking
   - 5G connectivity and connected vehicles
   - Electronics and software in automotive industry

4. **Electronics & Hardware Lab**
   - IoT and home automation
   - Resources: Bread Boards, Arduino Boards, Sensors
   - Building IoT solutions
   - Home automation systems integration

5. **Robotics & Coding Lab**
   - Basic principles of coding and robotics
   - Tools: Make-wonder Dash Robotics, Tanks Coding kits
   - Engaging learning experience for beginners in AI and coding

6. **Arm Ecosystem Lab**
   - Collaboration with Arm
   - Leveraging Arm's architecture in automotive and IoT sectors
   - Platform for demonstrating Arm's capabilities
   - African context projects

7. **EC Film Hub**
   - Partnership with National Film and Video Foundation
   - Post-production in film and media
   - Space for local filmmakers
   - High-end feature film production and editing

8. **E-sports & Gaming Club**
   - Connect local gamers to international competitions
   - Creative space for 3D animators and software developers
   - African-inspired games development

### Key Features:
- Collaborative community for startups
- Tailored support and mentorship
- Access to funding opportunities
- Sustainable solutions focus
- Expertise in product development, marketing, and fundraising
- Free programs for selected startups

## 2. CHEMIN

### Description
A not-for-profit business incubator under the Small Enterprise Development Agency's (SEDA) program, specializing in the downstream chemicals industry. Supports early-stage technology-based businesses in the chemical sector.

### Products & Capabilities

#### Services Offered:
1. **Incubation Models**
   - Enterprise development support
   - Early-stage business support

2. **Laboratory Services**
   - Metals testing
   - Detergents testing
   - Water testing
   - Coal testing
   - Petroleum products testing
   - Pharmaceutical tests

3. **Technical Services**
   - Product development technology
   - Technology transfer
   - Collaboration with universities, experts, financing agencies, and government departments

4. **Resources Provided:**
   - Lab space
   - Testing facilities
   - Manufacturing equipment
   - Office space
   - Seed finance
   - University collaborations
   - Expert networks
   - Financing agency connections
   - Government department linkages

### Focus Areas:

## 3. ECITI (Eastern Cape Information Technology Institute)

### Description
A non-profit business incubator established by the Eastern Cape Development Corporation (ECDC) in 2004, specializing in the information communications technology (ICT) and film sectors. Moved into the STP on July 20, 2012.

### Products & Capabilities

#### Services Offered:
1. **Infrastructure Support**
   - Office space and facilities
   - Technology infrastructure

2. **Mentorship & Training**
   - Business mentorship
   - Technical training programs
   - Industry-specific guidance

3. **Network Linkages**
   - Industry network connections
   - Academic network connections
   - Information resources

4. **Focus Areas:**
   - Information Communications Technology (ICT)
   - Film sector
   - Small, Medium, and Micro Enterprises (SMMEs)
   - Start-up support
   - Business failure risk reduction

## 4. ECNGOC (Eastern Cape NGO Coalition)

### Description
A representative structure for civil society organizations (NGOs and CBOs) in the Eastern Cape, focused on socio-economic transformation for marginalized communities.

### Products & Capabilities

#### Services Offered:
1. **Organizational Capacity Building**
   - Strengthening organizational capacity for development activities
   - NGO legislation and compliance support
   - Sustainability initiatives

2. **Advocacy & Influence**
   - Influencing relations between civil society, local government, and communities
   - Promoting asset-based community-driven development (ABCD)

3. **Leadership Development**
   - Citizen-oriented leadership development
   - Accountability and engagement training

4. **Focus Areas:**
   - Socio-economic transformation
   - Marginalized communities
   - Civil society representation
   - Community-driven development
